Cross out pairs of numbers until the board is empty — two numbers match when they are equal or add up to ten. The puzzle you know from countless ad-stuffed apps, rebuilt the calm way: no ads, no timer, no lives, no way to lose. A stuck board simply ends with its score.

THREE MODES
• Free play — your board, your rules: pick a seed (any word works), your add and hint budgets, and an optional score to beat.
• Daily Knobel — one board per day, the same for everyone. Missed days wait patiently in the calendar; nothing is ever lost.
• Adventure — 50 measured levels in five chapters, from very easy to very hard. Each solved level unlocks the next.

CHALLENGE SOMEONE
Share any board as a QR code or link: same board, same rules, your score as the target. No account, no server — the whole challenge fits in the code.

CALM BY DESIGN
Hints never cost points. Undo takes back any move, as far as you like. Background music you can shape: switch off any track you dislike — the rotation simply skips it.

FOR ALL EYES
Dark and light themes, text scaling up to 200 %, and typefaces for dyslexia and low vision — the digits are huge anyway. In English, German, French and Italian.

YOUR DATA STAYS YOURS
Fully offline. No accounts, no analytics, no ads, no purchases, no data collection. Free and open source — part of the fuchs family of calm apps (fuchsnest.ch).
